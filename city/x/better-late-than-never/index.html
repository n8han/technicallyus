Better late than never...

By Doctor Barnett, on January 23, 2007

So, as I have previously mentioned, Pittsburgh is just a little bit behind the times. However, when we embrace something, we embrace it right. Some might argue that the cupcake craze is over, but we really don’t care. We are in the middle of a full on cupcake WAR, and while I am not a general (thank you Krissie and Lee) I would like to think of myself of at least a corporal. (I can apparently ganache a mean cupcake!) Coco’s Cupcake Cafe is the real deal. I know the food side of things tends to be Mr. Little Pants’ milileu, however I feel connected, and having tasted both before I was affiliated with either, feel passionately that Coco’s makes a superior product, and therefore feel the need to share it with the world!
