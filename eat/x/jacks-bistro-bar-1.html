Jack's Bistro & Bar

By Leland, on November 5, 2005

Bad news for the peace-loving residents of East 11th Street at University Place: the former site of our beloved Lemongrass has announced its new tenant: Jack’s Bistro and Bar. What could be worse? We’ve been blissfully spared from bar noise and hear little at night other than garbage trucks and car alarms. But you can bet that once this joint opens, there will be even more drunk frat boys crawling around University Place and spilling onto 11th. Wo Bro indeed.
