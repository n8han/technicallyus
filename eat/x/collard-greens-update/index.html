Collard greens update

By Leland, on January 28, 2006 { freshdirect }

All in all, FreshDirect is a decent company. They always solve their problems by throwing cash at people!

Dear MrLittlePants,

Thank you for contacting FreshDirect. We are very sorry to hear of this overage. We will issue a credit back for the difference and apologize for any inconvenience.

We have issued you the following credits for your order: $2.09 from the Vegetables Department in store credit.

FreshDirect

For once, “inconvenience” is an appropriate word. You should see those greens in my tiny fridge!
