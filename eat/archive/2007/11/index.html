A few of us went to Blue Hill at Stone Barns yesterday. I put some non-food pictures here. (The dining room didn’t feel camera-friendly, and I hate to be that guy.) All in all, it was a perfect experience, from beginning to end. But I couldn’t help feeling like it was all too perfect, too Manhattan, and much too expensive, considering the hassle and cost of just getting to Tarrytown. I ate fresh greens with mushrooms and a poached, fried egg on top, followed by a confit of Berkshire pork and then chocolate bread pudding. All was extremely delicious and rather small. I’ll go again if someone else is treating.
